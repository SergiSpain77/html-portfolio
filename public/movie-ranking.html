The Best Movies According to Sergi

My top 3 movies of all-time (not really, but I like them).

Licorice Pizza (2021)

This bittersweet love story is a masterpiece of storytelling, and it's impossible not to feel alive while watching it.

The Lighthouse (2019)

A powerplay of acting and directing. Things are not what they seem but everything's been there from the beginning. Best movie of the last 10 years.

The Favourite (2018)

Lanthimos at its best.
